Lordrick Kahinga

PhD Scholar,Astronomy and Astrophysics, UC Santa Cruz

Hi! My name is Lordrick, I was born in Arusha, Tanzania, and studied physics at the University of Dodoma, Tanzania. I graduated in 2022 and moved to the USA in 2023 to pursue a PhD in Astronomy and Astrophysics at the University of California Santa Cruz.

I am passionate about using signals to explore the structure of the universe. Currently, my main focus is on Fast Radio Bursts (FRBs) and how they can help us to study baryon distributions and trace dark matter in galactic halos. I am also interested in using FRBs as a way to understand the cosmos.

Although there were few research opportunities in my home country, my passion for astronomy drove me to seek opportunities internationally. Fortunately, I was warmly welcomed and given the chance to pursue my lifelong dream of becoming an astrophysicist. I hope to inspire students, particularly those from underrepresented communities, to never give up on their aspirations.
