About Me

Hello there! I am pursuing a Ph.D. in Astronomy and Astrophysics at the prestigious UC Santa Cruz, where I am delving deep into the fascinating world of Fast Radio Bursts (FRBs) to explore the Circumgalactic Medium (CGM) of nearby galaxies. I am also interested in uncovering the mysteries of the universe and shed light on the complexities of cosmology through the study of Damped Lyman-Alpha systems (DLAs). With a passion for unraveling the secrets of the cosmos and a commitment to excellence in my field, I am dedicated to making groundbreaking contributions to the scientific community.
