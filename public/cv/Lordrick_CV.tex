\section{Research Interests}

\textbf{Fast Radio Bursts (FRBs):} Host galaxy demographics; FRB progenitor theory; optical spectroscopic follow-up; FRBs as probes of the CGM and IGM; baryon census; cosmological parameter estimation.

\textbf{Circumgalactic \& Intergalactic Medium:} Probing diffuse baryons in nearby galaxy halos using FRB dispersion measures; machine learning approaches to constrain baryon density in the CGM/IGM.

\textbf{Galaxy Formation \& Evolution:} Quasar absorption lines; Damped Lyman-alpha systems (DLAs); physics of the interstellar and intergalactic medium.
